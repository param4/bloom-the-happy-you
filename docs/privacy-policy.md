# Bloom - The Happy You — Privacy Policy

_Effective 11 July 2026_

Bloom ("the app") is a personal wellbeing journal published by GM Studios - Learn & Grow. It is built so that your inner world stays private.

## What we store, and where

Everything you create in Bloom — journal entries, voice notes, videos, photos, mood check-ins, to-do lists, and your streak — is stored **only on your device**. This content is never uploaded to us or to any third party, and we have no way to access it.

## No account, nothing to sign up for

Bloom does not require — or currently offer — an account. There is no sign-up, no email address to provide, and no profile stored on any server. The app collects **no personal data** at all.

## No tracking

Bloom contains no advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no trackers. We do not sell, share, or monetize any data.

## Permissions the app requests

- **Camera & microphone** — only while you record a video or voice moment.
- **Photos** — only when you choose an image for your vision board.
- **Notifications** — only for the daily reminder you schedule.

Each permission is optional and requested only when you first use the related feature. Declining a permission never blocks the rest of the app.

## Deleting your data

- **Clear data** (profile menu) permanently deletes all content in the app, including recorded video, audio, and image files.
- Uninstalling the app deletes all locally stored data.

Because nothing you create ever leaves your device, there is no copy held anywhere else to request deletion of.

## Children

Bloom is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.
