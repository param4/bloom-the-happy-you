Bloom is a gentle, private space to notice your own happiness — and grow more of it.

No account. No ads. No cloud. Everything you create stays on your phone, for your eyes only.

🌸 CHECK IN WITH YOURSELF
Log your mood in seconds with a soft daily check-in. Over time, Bloom helps you see the shape of your days.

📓 JOURNAL YOUR WAY
Write it, speak it, or film it. Capture entries as text, voice notes, or little videos — whatever feels natural in the moment.

🎥 THE JOY BOOTH
Record short videos of your happiest moments — a laugh, a sunset, a small win — and save them for the future you who'll need them.

Having a heavy day? One tap resurfaces a happy memory you saved earlier. Your past self becomes your own best friend.

✨ DREAM BOARD & MANIFESTATION MOMENTS
Build a vision board of the life you're growing toward. Add photos to your dreams, and let Bloom surprise you with quiet moments to reconnect with them.

✅ GENTLE TO-DOS
Small, kind to-do lists that celebrate what you did — not what you didn't.

🔥 STREAKS THAT FORGIVE
Show up daily and watch your streak bloom. Miss a day? Bloom has grace built in — no guilt, ever.

🎨 MAKE IT YOURS
Choose your accent theme — warm terracotta, calm sage, or soft blush — and set a daily reminder that suits your rhythm.

🔒 TRULY PRIVATE, BY DESIGN

Bloom isn't about fixing you — you were never broken. It's about noticing the good that's already blooming, one small moment at a time.

Plant a moment today. 🌱
